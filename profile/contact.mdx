## Updating Your Contact Address

You can update your contact address in your profile settings. This address is used for official communication and may be required for organizational records.

**Note:** Your contact address will only be visible to administrators within your organization. Other members will not have access to this information.

## How to Update Your Contact Address

1. Go to your profile settings page.
2. Locate the section for contact information.
3. Enter your updated contact address.
4. Save your changes.

Administrators will be able to view your contact address for administrative purposes, but it will remain private from other users.
