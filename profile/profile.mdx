# Profile Settings Overview

On this page, you can view your profile settings within the organization. The following information is displayed:

- **Email Address:** Your registered email for this organization.
- **Job Title:** Your current job title as assigned by the organization.
- **Titanbase Role:** Your role within Titanbase (e.g., Member, Admin, etc.).

These settings are **read-only** and cannot be changed by you directly. Any updates to your email address, job title, or Titanbase role must be made by your organization administrators.
