---
title: "Verify a Phone Number"
description: "This guide will tell you how to add and verify a number so you can receive SMS notifications."
---

<Note>
  You must verify your phone number for each organization you are a part to receive messages from them. If you are a member of Org A and Org B and only verify your number with Org A, you will only be eligible to receive notifications for Org A until you also verify your number with Org B.
</Note>

## Add a Phone Number to Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to your Agency Profile by clicking on your user name and avatar.">
    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-08-30at8.29.40PM.png"
      alt="Agency Profile Dropdown"
      title="Agency Profile Dropdown"
      className="mr-auto"
      style={{ width:"34%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Mobile Phone Number">
    Click on the "Notifications Tab" to reach this screen.

    <img
      src="/images/image.png"
      alt="image.png"
      title="image.png"
      style={{ width:"80%" }}
    />

    <Info>
      Only US (\+1) numbers are accepted. You will not be able to verify unless you use \+1XXXXXXXXXX format.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title='Click on "Send Me a Code"'>
    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-08-30at8.39.26PM.png"
      alt="Send a code"
      title="Send a Code"
      style={{ width:"48%" }}
    />

    This button only appears if you are using the correct format for your phone number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Your Phone for a Code & Enter It">
    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-08-30at8.41.46PM.png"
      alt="Enter verification code"
      title="Enter verification code"
      style={{ width:"59%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make Sure You are Verified">
    If verification is successful you will receive a text message with the following message.

    > [Titanbase] You have successfully verified your phone number. Reply STOP to opt out of these messages at any time.

    You should also see a verified mark beside your phone number.

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-08-30at8.44.53PM.png"
      alt="Verified Mark"
      title="Verified Mark"
      style={{ width:"54%" }}
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Update or Verify an Existing Number

If you already have a number on your user profile click on "Update Number" to verify it.

<img
  src="/images/Screenshot2025-08-30at8.46.22PM.png"
  alt="Update number"
  title="Update number"
  style={{ width:"57%" }}
/>

Enter your phone number in the \+1XXXXXXXXXX format and follow the verification prompts.
